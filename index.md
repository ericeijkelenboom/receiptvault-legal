# Reci — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-03-18
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-19

## What Reci Does

Reci is a receipt scanning app. You photograph receipts, and the app extracts structured data (store name, date, items, total) and stores it on your device.

## Data We Collect

### Receipt Images
When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to our server for processing. The image is used solely to extract text and is **not stored on our servers**. Processing is stateless — images are discarded immediately after extraction.

### Receipt Data
Extracted receipt data (store name, date, items, total) is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not have access to your receipts.

### Crash Reports
We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. This includes device type, OS version, and crash stack traces. No personal data is included. This data is used solely to fix bugs and improve app stability.

### Analytics
We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., how often receipts are scanned). TelemetryDeck is privacy-first: **no personal data is collected, no user tracking occurs, and no advertising identifiers are used**. No consent banner is required.

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Sent |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Anthropic Claude | Receipt data extraction from images | Receipt image (not stored) |
| Firebase Crashlytics | Crash reporting | Anonymous device/crash data |
| TelemetryDeck | Usage analytics | Anonymous event signals |

## What We Do NOT Collect
- No account or login required
- No email addresses, names, or personal identifiers
- No location data
- No contacts or calendar data
- No advertising identifiers
- No data is sold to third parties

## Data Storage
All receipt data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We do not operate a cloud database for user data.

## Your Rights
Since all receipt data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete individual receipts or remove all data by deleting the app.
